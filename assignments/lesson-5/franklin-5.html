Franklin Breaks Record

Sun and temperatures over 70°F brought up a new record-braking Winter in the city of Franklin. What was expected to be a chilli, snow-filled winter, has turned into an early Spring showcase - minus the flowers. The valley is green, residents are enjoying outdoor activities, and the winds are just a comfortable breeze. For the city of Franklin, this has been the hottest Winter in over fifty years!
